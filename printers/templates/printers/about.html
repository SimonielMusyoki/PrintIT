About PrintIT

PrintIT Information & History:

We are an online Printing service to connect custoers to printing services.
PrintIT was founded by Munge Ben (Electrical Engineer) and Musyoki Mutuku (Computer Programmer)
The two met and saw a problem that persisted in the society: Having to move from buildings with high floors and moving all over the town to look for printers.
They then saw the need for a solution to do a computerised printing Syste which they named PrintIT - a name that came up as a typo of Printing Inforation Technology and became popular in Nairobi CBD.
This system allows printing business people to register their services and can be seen by custoers near them This cut down costs and tie taken to search for a printing service and also allow for delivery iddlemen so that a person doesnt have to move from office
